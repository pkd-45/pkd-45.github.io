Key projects
SAMI Spinny Angular momentum

A project linking galaxy angular momentum to structure in the SAMI Galaxy Survey, including deprojection, rotation-curve fitting, cumulative j profiles, and uncertainty-aware measurements.

Simba ML Halo-to-galaxy

Building machine-learning frameworks that predict galaxy properties from dark-matter halo information, connecting simulations to observations in a scalable and physically interpretable way.

Hector FAST H I

Exploring how resolved optical kinematics relate to global H I properties, with proposed FAST follow-up aimed at calibrating low-mass and intermediate-mass systems.

MUSE IFU cubes Extraction

Developing tools to extract physically meaningful signals from IFU data cubes, including globular cluster spectra, outflows, metallicity-gradient workflows, and survey-scale emission-line products.
